Partitions

$\mathbf E = (E_1, E_2, \ldots, E_N)$
$E_k \subseteq \mathbb R^d$, $\lvert E_k \cap E_j \rvert = 0$ for $k \neq j$,
$\left\vert \mathbb R^d \setminus \displaystyle\bigcup_{k=1}^N E_k \right\vert = 0$

isoperimetric partitions

$$\mathrm{Per}(\mathbf E, B) = \frac 1 2 \sum_{k=1}^N \mathrm{Per}(E_k, B)$$
